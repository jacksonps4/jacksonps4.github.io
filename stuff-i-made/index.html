Stuff I have made

I spend some of my spare time building things. My focus tends to be functionality over presentation - first make it work and then make it pretty but when you have a limited amount of time to spend on something, you tend to just make it work.

Indigenously Rail

This consumes the Network Rail and National Rail feeds to provide real-time track position information, schedule information and platform reminders at the origin station.

Weather data

The Met Office Datapoint API provides much free weather data. This app shows recent observations and can be searched.

Land registry

Want to find out how much your neighbours paid for their house? Search the land registry records to see!
